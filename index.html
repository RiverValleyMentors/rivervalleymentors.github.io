Frequently Asked Questions

What does "RivVal" mean and how does it work? +

RivVal stands for "River Valley" but also represents "rival" - our philosophy of helping students rival their best selves. We use competitive strategies and progress tracking to motivate students to outperform their previous achievements, not compete against others.

How do online tutoring sessions work? +

We use Google Meets for all online sessions. You'll receive a meeting link before each session. All you need is a computer, tablet, or phone with internet access. Digital tools are used to share notes and work through problems together in real-time.

What's the difference between packages? +

Drop-in sessions offer flexibility at $45 each. The Growth Challenger (12 sessions) saves you $60 and includes progress tracking. The Excellence Mastery (24 sessions) saves $168, includes 2 bonus sessions, and offers premium features like custom study plans and quarterly parent consultations.

What if my child needs help in multiple subjects? +

Both math and English tutoring are available, so we can work on both subjects within the same session or alternate between subjects across different sessions. A customized plan is created based on each student's specific needs and competitive goals.

How does the Rival & Refer program work? +

When you refer a friend who books their first package, you receive a $25 credit toward your next session and your friend gets $20 off their first package. Just mention the referral when they book their intro session! Help them rival their limits too.

How do you track progress and competition with myself? +

Personalized progress reports track improvement in specific skills and concepts, showing how you're rivaling your previous performance. Parents receive regular updates showing their child's strengths, areas for improvement, and competitive achievements.

Do you help with homework or focus on competitive strategies? +

Both! We help with specific homework assignments and teach underlying concepts with competitive learning strategies. The goal is to help students become independent learners who can rival top performers and tackle new challenges confidently.

What makes RivVal different from other tutors? +

RivVal uses competitive strategies to motivate self-improvement rather than competition with others. As a current university student, I understand recent curriculum changes and effective study techniques. My economics background provides a unique perspective on both academic success and real-world application.

Can I pay for packages in installments? +

Yes! The Growth Challenger can be split into 3 payments of $165, and the Excellence Mastery can be split into 4 payments of $240. Contact us to set up a payment plan that works for your family's budget.

How flexible is the scheduling? +

Very flexible! Scheduling works around students' school schedules, extracurricular activities, and family commitments. Package students get consistent weekly slots, but timing can be adjusted as needed. Drop-in sessions can be booked with short notice.
